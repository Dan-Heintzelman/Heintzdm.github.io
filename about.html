Bio

Hello Planet. (Trying not to be cliche here). My name is Dan Heintzelman. I am first and foremost what one might call, a geek...and proud of it. My whole life I have been intrigued by what makes things work. Yes, I was that kid that put crayons in the VCR (remember those?). Not because I wanted to torture my parents, but because I've always been curious. Even though my niche is tech, I am fueled by a deep passion for music and the arts. I play guitar, djembe (African hand drum), and even a little bit on the set. I am from Cincinnati, but I live in Chicago. Even though I live close to home, and like being in the midwest, I love to travel. I have worked in L.A., Orlando (Disney Imagineering), Italy (Study Abroad), and various other places. So, you might ask....what do I do?

I have a Bachelor's Degree in Urban Planning from the University of Cincinnati. You may ask why this big tech geek was studying city planning.
